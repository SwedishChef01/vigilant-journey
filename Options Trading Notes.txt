OPTIONS TRADING NOTES

# There are two types of options: calls and puts.
# The price of a call option rises when its underlying stock goes up.
# The price of a put option rises when its underlying stock goes down.
# Owning a call option gives you the right to buy a certain stock at a specific price
  (strike price) by a certain date.
# Owning a put option gives you the right to sell a certain stock at a specific price
  (strike price) by a certain date.
# Option price above strike price = ITM = In the Money
# Option price below strike price = OTM = Out of the Money
# The number of existing contracts for an option is known as open interest.
